📖 How to Play

🐜 Goal: Build a thriving ant colony. Gather food, hatch workers, train soldiers, and defeat enemies.

🌾 Food: Workers collect food from stations. Use food to build chambers and buy upgrades.

🥚 Eggs: The queen lays eggs. They hatch into worker ants. Build a Nursery to speed up hatching.

⚔️ Combat: Spiders invade in waves. Build Soldier Chambers to defend. Rally boosts speed temporarily.

💎 Gems: Earned from scouts, achievements, and bosses. Spend in the Gem Shop for powerful items.

🧬 Evolution: Unlock after building Research Chamber (Lv8). Upgrade ants permanently.

✨ Prestige: At Level 30, reset your colony for Prestige Points and powerful milestones.

⬆️ Ascension: After Prestige 100, gain Ascension Points for permanent multipliers and ultimate upgrades.

🌍 Zones: Scouts unlock new zones with unique enemies and bosses. Tap the zone name to switch.
