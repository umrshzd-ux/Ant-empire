📖 How to Play

🐜 Goal: Build a thriving ant colony.

🌾 Food: Workers collect food from stations.

🥚 Eggs: Queen lays eggs that hatch into workers.

⚔️ Combat: Spiders invade in waves. Build Soldiers.

💎 Gems: Earned from scouts, achievements, bosses.

🧬 Evolution: Upgrade ants permanently.

✨ Prestige: Reset at Lv30 for bonuses.

⬆️ Ascension: Ultimate prestige at P100.

🌍 Zones: Scouts unlock new areas.
